About Nexus

We build the infrastructure that keeps buildings intelligent.

Nexus Facilities Group was founded to solve the fragmentation problem in commercial building management. Before Nexus, operators juggled dozens of disconnected systems — separate dashboards for HVAC, water, access control, and lighting, none of them talking to each other.

Our platform changes that. A single broker. A unified API. One dashboard for your entire portfolio. From a 10-floor office block to a 40-building commercial estate, Nexus gives you complete visibility and control from anywhere in the world.

Our IoT layer connects directly to the physical infrastructure of every building we manage — thermostats, pump controllers, environmental sensors, and more — streaming live telemetry around the clock so your team can respond before problems become incidents.
